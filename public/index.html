Story

Prof Daniel Lim is feeling overwhelmed as he is teaching the 2 challenging modules - CS424-Image Perception & COR-IS1702 Computational Thinking. He needs to de-stress but currently all recreational and entertainment places are closed and citizen are strongly encouraged to stay home.

There's a group of student who came up with this game where prof can do yoga to de-stress, it can be played anywhere, any time. All you need is your laptop and webcam. It allows multiple players too! Hence Prof Daniel can do yoga with other SMU Profs and his wife :)
